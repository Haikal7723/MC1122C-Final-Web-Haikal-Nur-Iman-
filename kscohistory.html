Was established on 15 October 2023 by a 16-year-old boy named Haikal from Selangor, Malaysia. It started when he randomly design a clothes and become addicted to designing.

One day, he was thinking if he can make all his design into a real life clothes and decided to produce one. The business idea came when he realize that he can make money from it. All thing started only on a personal computer in his room.

The name KillStealz originally a common word use by gamers. The name was choosen because of Haikal's gaming addiction. He also shorten the name into KSCO for an easier pronounciation where 'KS' stand for KillStealz and 'CO' stand for Company.
